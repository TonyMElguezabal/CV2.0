## Problem

Oracle's Database Engineering organization relied on multiple internal
applications to manage the software development lifecycle, centered on
a proprietary source control management (SCM) platform similar in
purpose to Git. Developers had to move between disconnected systems to
track assignments, requirements, workflow status, and dependencies,
creating fragmentation and operational overhead. The goal was to give
developers a single web-based hub consolidating the most important
information from these systems into one operational dashboard.

## Approach

Taking ADEHub from concept to General Availability required aligning
stakeholders, architecture, security, budget, and delivery teams around
a shared vision — sequencing and alignment mattered as much as
execution.

## Outcome

Led the platform from initial concept through General Availability,
consolidating SCM workflows, requirements, and activities from multiple
disconnected systems into a single developer hub.
