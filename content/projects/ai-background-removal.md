## Problem

Removing backgrounds from images was a manual editing step for Placeit
users, with no native, automated way to do it on the platform.

## Approach

Led the delivery of an AI-powered background removal tool, coordinating
the training and integration of a machine-learning image-segmentation
model for automatic, precise subject extraction — part of a broader
content-editing tool suite guided by usage data and direct customer
feedback rather than assumptions about what customers wanted.

## Outcome

The tool replaced a manual background-removal step with a fully
automated one, and — as part of the same tool suite — contributed to
growing overall platform adoption by approximately 6%, adding nearly
10,000 users.
