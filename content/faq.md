# Frequently Asked Questions

### Who is Jose?

Jose is a Technical Delivery Manager who leads complex software programs —
cloud migrations, AI-enabled products, and cross-functional engineering
initiatives — pairing hands-on technical depth with delivery discipline.

### Has he led cloud migrations?

Yes. At Acme Corp he led the migration of a monolithic order-processing
system to eight independently deployable microservices, moving deployment
frequency from biweekly to daily with no customer-facing outage.

### How does he handle underperforming teams?

He rebuilds trust through smaller, verifiable milestones paired with
frequent stakeholder visibility — at Acme Corp this restored on-time
delivery within one quarter after a team had missed its prior two
deadlines.
