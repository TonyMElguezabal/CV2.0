technical support through senior delivery leadership — from diagnosing

distributed engineering portfolios at TCS, to leading AI-enabled
platform delivery at Oracle. He pairs technical depth with delivery
discipline across industries including banking, aviation, e-commerce,
and enterprise cloud infrastructure.

major bank — recovering an unbaselined scope into 100% milestone

clearer ownership and escalation. He's also consolidated Oracle's
fragmented internal SCM tooling into a single platform that reached
